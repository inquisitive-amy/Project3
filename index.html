Get your web project done while creating up-and-coming developer talent.

For centuries, journeypeople have been incredibly important members of the economy. They’re folks who are fully educated in a trade or craft, but not yet masters. After completing an apprenticeship, these journeypeople would travel from town to town, taking on different projects and gaining experience. Eventually, they would be invited to settle and become that town’s master. And the cycle continued.

At JourneyMade, we’re taking our own spin on the journeyperson tradition and applying it to web development. Under the guidance of Brenna O’Brien and Christina Truong (our resident masters) and after nine weeks of full-time training at HackerYou’s front-end development immersive, our team members are ready and willing to work on your projects. One day they’ll leave us to join another company full-time. And that’s kind of the point.

Why JourneyMade?

A team for every budget

We specialize in web projects between $1000 and $5000, and can also take on smaller tasks at our standard rate of $50 an hour.

Paired Programming

Two minds are better than one! We put two developers to work on every project or task. They learn more, and you get a better result.

Experts Available

Brenna O’Brien, Christina Truong and Jared Phillips are available to assist with client projects anytime. It ensures your project is done right, the first time.

Get your project done right

Finding a great freelance web developer isn’t easy. And there are plenty of horror stories about projects gone wrong. At JourneyMade, you’re hiring a team of developers trained right, with the resources to make sure your project is a success.

Want to learn to do it yourself? We do that, too.

Would you rather learn how to code your project yourself? We’re happy to have our team members teach you. It’s $50 an hour.

Client Education

Note that, before we’ll accept you as a client, you may be required to attend a two-hour workshop (either in-person or online) on how websites work, what web developers do, and how your web project will come together. We’ll even get you writing some HTML & CSS!
